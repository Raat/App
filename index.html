About Windmill Windup

The Windmill Windup is a yearly ultimate frisbee tournament held in Amsterdam, comprised of three divisions of play (open, women's and mixed). The three day event is loaded with ultimate frisbee action and off the field antics, resulting in an unforgettable weekend of intense sport and constant fun.

7 rules of ultimate frisbee

The game of Ultimate frisbee is played by the following rules:

  1. 7 players on the line
  2. First team to 15 wins
  3. Teams switch sides after each score
  4. Half time once a team scores 8 goals
  5. Players may not run with the disc
  6. Players must pass the disc within 10 seconds
  7. Games are self officiating

Final ranking poule day

Teams will play a round-robin in four pools. The best 2 teams of each pool play a bracket for the win. The final ranking of the pool are based on:

  1. Total of games won
  2. [if teams are equal] Result of games against eachother
  3. [if teams are equal] Total of scored points - lost points
  4. [if teams are equal] Flip a coin
